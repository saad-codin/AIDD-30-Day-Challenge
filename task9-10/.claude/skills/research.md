# Research Skill

Gather comprehensive research material for book topics and chapters.

## Purpose
This skill helps you research and compile information from various sources to create well-informed, factual content for your book.

## Instructions
When using this skill:
1. Identify the main topic or subject matter
2. Break down the topic into key areas to research
3. Gather relevant information and sources
4. Organize findings into a structured format
5. Create an outline of the research for easy reference

## Usage
Ask Claude to research a book topic by providing:
- The main subject matter
- Target audience level (beginner/intermediate/advanced)
- Key aspects to focus on
- Any specific sources or types of information needed

## Example
"Research the history of artificial intelligence for a technical book aimed at developers. Focus on: key milestones, influential figures, technological breakthroughs, and current trends."

## Output Format
The skill will return:
- Topic overview
- Key research findings
- Organized by subtopics
- Relevant sources and references
- Suggested chapter topics based on research
