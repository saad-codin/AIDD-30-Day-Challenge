# Content Editor Skill

Edit, refine, and improve book content for clarity, style, and impact.

## Purpose
This skill provides comprehensive editing and refinement of book content, ensuring professional quality, consistency, and readability throughout your work.

## Instructions
When using this skill:
1. Review the content for grammar and spelling
2. Check for clarity and readability
3. Ensure consistency in tone and style
4. Improve sentence structure and flow
5. Verify factual accuracy
6. Strengthen arguments and examples
7. Remove redundancy and wordiness
8. Enhance overall impact and engagement

## Usage
Ask Claude to edit your content by providing:
- The text to be edited
- Specific focus areas (grammar, clarity, tone, etc.)
- Target reading level
- Style guide or tone preferences
- Any specific issues to address
- Word count constraints if applicable

## Example
"Edit this chapter for clarity and professional tone. Target audience: business professionals. Focus on: removing jargon, improving sentence flow, strengthening conclusions, and adding relevant statistics."

## Output Format
The skill will provide:
- Edited version of the content
- List of changes made with explanations
- Suggestions for further improvement
- Clarity and readability score
- Style consistency notes
- Strength assessment of arguments
